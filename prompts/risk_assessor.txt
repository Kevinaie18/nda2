<task>
Calculate NDA risk score (0-100) using the weighted scoring matrix below. Show your calculation.
</task>

<scoring_algorithm>
BASE_SCORE = 40

FACTORS:
1. NON_COMPETE (weight: 25 points max)
   - Not present: 0
   - ≤1 year, limited geography: -5
   - 1-2 years, regional: +5
   - 2-3 years, broad: +15
   - >3 years or global: +25

2. LIABILITY (weight: 25 points max)
   - Mutual and capped: -5
   - One-sided but capped: +5
   - Uncapped but limited scope: +15
   - Unlimited/uncapped: +25

3. JURISDICTION (weight: 20 points max)
   - Local courts (same as I&P): -5
   - OHADA or regional arbitration: 0
   - Neutral arbitration (ICC, LCIA): +5
   - Foreign exclusive courts: +15
   - Unfavorable foreign (e.g., China exclusive): +20

4. CONFIDENTIALITY (weight: 15 points max)
   - Standard with all exceptions: -3
   - Broad but with key exceptions: +5
   - Overly broad, few exceptions: +10
   - Perpetual or no exceptions: +15

5. TERMINATION (weight: 15 points max)
   - Mutual termination with 30 days: -5
   - Unilateral with notice: +5
   - Difficult/expensive to exit: +10
   - No termination clause: +15

FINAL_SCORE = BASE_SCORE + SUM(all factors)
Clamp to range [0, 100]
</scoring_algorithm>

- NO markdown, NO explanation, ONLY the CSV line
</output_format>
